About自己紹介

Hello, my name is Keikichi Den. After graduating from university, I joined AGC Inc., where I was responsible for IT-related tasks across various locations in China. My work included server maintenance, printer troubleshooting, device setup, translating technical documents, and teaching basic IT courses. I also led RPA development and automation using Python, improving system efficiency as part of our digital transformation efforts. Later, missing life in Japan, I decided to move back in October 2023 and joined Nippon Express Co., Ltd. At Nippon Express, I focused on system operations, automation with RPA and batch scripts, data processing and analysis using Python, as well as EDI data mapping. I also provided IT support, including network troubleshooting and verifying printer drivers. To continuously stay ahead of the latest trends in the IT industry, aiming to become a top-tier global technologist, I joined Lenovo Japan in November 2024, where I was responsible for customizing and testing enterprise Windows master images, providing specialized consulting on AMD and Nvidia driver implementation for enterprise clients including ISVs, offering advice on AI customization and enterprise AI security, and contributing to achieving optimal compatibility and robust stability in customer environments as a Windows expert. I joined ANA Systems in August 2026. Since I was a kid, I've always been fascinated by aviation, so being part of the ANA Group means a lot to me. I'm excited to use my IT background to contribute to ANA's vision of "Uniting the World in Wonder" and keep growing as an engineer.
